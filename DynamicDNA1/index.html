Modus Data and Architecture Company Profile



Modus Data and Architecture also known as Modus DNA is an empowerment company aimed at assisting companies within the ICT sector the bridge the gap between the huge scarcity of skills within the sector and the young minds that come into the sector with no experience.

We are 51% black female owned entity making us a Level 2 b-bbee status and an empowering supplier. Modus DNA is under the DVT umbrella. We spend a lot of time researching about the skills shortage within the ICT Sector and have seen an imbalance within the sector as far as education and practical skills.Our target audience are the youth who would like to take a career path within the sector.

After doing much research we have seen that we can be the driving tool to bridge that gap by moulding and grooming our own talent within the identified scarce skills in our country.

We are a training company we empower young individuals with business, professional skills as well as practical skills in relation to the qualification they have covered so as to better prepare them for what’s to come in the workplace.

Our Mission and Vision is to assist the young into gaining access into the workplace with the confidence that they will be able to deliver the reverse is also true that the employer is confident in taking the young into the workplace knowing that will be able to deliver on the tasks given o a daily basis while working there.
